USB-C

The European Union will require devices to include a USB-C port for charging, by the end of 20241.

I think the USB-C connector is much better than previous USB connectors. Especially USB Micro-B. I don’t think it’s the best connector, however. USB-C cables are a mess, and the USB Implementers Forum is still hopeless at creating clear, consistent, consumer-friendly names2.

I’ll miss Lightning when Apple is forced to remove it from a future version of their products.

  1. There are some exceptions: USB-C is only required for devices that support wired charging, and laptops won’t be required to include USB-C until Spring 2026. ↩︎

  2. USB 3.2 Gen 1 ↩︎
